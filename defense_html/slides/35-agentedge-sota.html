Why ML Baselines?
why not 6G rows
1 · NOT FULL AGENTIC FRAMEWORKS
The 6G papers are valuable related work, but they do not satisfy full PARES, so in this thesis they are not classified as complete agentic frameworks.
2 · DO NOT FIT OUR SYSTEM MODEL
They do not instantiate the validated Intent-Observe-Plan-Act agentic loop that AgentEdge evaluates for edge-cloud orchestration.
3 · WHY REACT AND LATS
They are actual agentic AI frameworks that interpret natural-language intent and coordinate multi-step actions, so they can be retargeted to the same tools, LLM, and scenarios for fair comparison.
