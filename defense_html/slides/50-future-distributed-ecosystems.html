two blockers · two directions
FW·3.A
Multi-agent Coordination
Parallel agents on shared infrastructure issue contradicting actions.
Direction
Intent-level consensus · lock-lease before commit · share plans, not raw state — scale by parallelising, not by stacking agents in series.
FW·3.B
Model-agnostic Design
Swap the provider — the tool calls change shape.
Direction
A canonical intent schema for agent tool-calls — an OpenAPI-equivalent — plus model-agnostic evaluation: choose LLMs on cost, latency, privacy, not compatibility.
Coordination · portability · systems research, not tool swaps
