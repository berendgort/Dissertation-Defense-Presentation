48/52   Synthesis

Reliable benchmarks for agentic orchestration

How do we benchmark an agent whose task has no unique correct answer?
Research directions
R1 · MULTI-SOLUTION BENCHMARKS
Define task-level equivalence classes of correct orchestration plans; expand LLM evaluation theory (BIG-Bench, HELM) into the systems domain where success is policy-valid, not string-equal.
R2 · FORMAL-METHODS GUARDRAILS
Fuse LLM planners with verifiable policy checkers (SMT / temporal logic over service graphs). The planner proposes; the checker certifies; the twin validates.
R3 · DOMAIN-SPECIFIC FINE-TUNES
Curate orchestration-trace datasets (plan, state, outcome); enable small LoRA/SFT models that are cheaper and more explainable than black-box frontier models.
Benchmarks · formal checkers · orchestration-specific models · not "swap the LLM"
