“Why not compare AERO against Informer directly?”
Informer
Attention-family survey (magazine paper). Not an AERO baseline: same footprint class as Pathformer.
Pathformer
Heavy accuracy frontier at 2.4 M params: the strongest baseline to beat on accuracy.
AERO
Small and accurate quadrant : matches Pathformer at ~4000× fewer parameters.
Three models, three roles. Matching the stronger heavy baseline is the harder claim.
