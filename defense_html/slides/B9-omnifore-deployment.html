Q·2 · Deployment granularity
OmniFORE is layer-agnostic by design.
We designed for the problem of generalisation, not for a particular deployment layer. The model choice on where it runs is an operator decision, not a research constraint.
Where can it be deployed · why
FAR EDGE
When the far edge has enough memory, e.g. aggregation nodes. Shares AERO's mission but with cross-service reach.
REGIONAL
The most natural host: serves many clusters, retraining cost amortised. Our evaluation uses this setting.
CLOUD
Cloud-hosted inference is also valid; latency is tolerable for forecasts at minute/hour horizons.
One-line answer: the question “where does it run?” is downstream of the research question “can one model generalise?”, which OmniFORE answers affirmatively at −30.4 % MAE.
