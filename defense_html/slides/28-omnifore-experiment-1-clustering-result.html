What changed
Same model, same tuning, same zero-shot test.
Only the training-trace selection changes.
What it means
Clustering forces the sample to cover bursty, steady, and periodic workload families.
Random picking over-samples the common patterns and misses the rare ones.
Why it matters
In this experiment, the improvement isolates the effect of the training-set selection step.
