Future Work 2 · Real-time Infrastructure

two blockers · two directions
FW·2.A
Inference Efficiency
Every orchestration = a chain of LLM calls. Tens of seconds to act.
Direction
Call-reduction via multi-step prompting · distilled orchestration-specific models right-sized per decision — the AERO lesson, applied to the planner.
FW·2.B
Tool Discovery & Semantic Search
Beyond ~30 tools, the planner picks the wrong one.
Direction
Semantic tool retrieval · hierarchical catalogs · autonomous read-only tool discovery — show the agent a focused set, not the whole shelf.
Latency budget · tool surface · context engineering · not “a faster LLM”
