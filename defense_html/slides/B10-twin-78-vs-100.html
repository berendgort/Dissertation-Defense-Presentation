Q·3 · Why 78.3 %, not 100 %?
78.3%
success rate · 60 runs
across 3 scales (8 / 20 / 35 nodes)
The twin is a gate, not an oracle.
Three residual failure modes (scoped in thesis)
F1 · TWIN-LIVE MISMATCH
The simulator approximates; plans validated in twin can still fail on the real system under extreme contention.
F2 · CRITIC RECALL
Critic LLM occasionally rubber-stamps an infeasible plan; recall is not 100 %. This is the biggest single contributor to the residual 21.7 %.
F3 · BUDGET CUT-OFF
Scenarios where the critic keeps rejecting and the refinement budget is exhausted before a feasible plan is found. By design: safe fail over silent accept.
Rehearsed framing: 78.3 % is the first validated success metric in this line of work; previous baselines (REACT / LATS) cannot even be measured consistently because they actuate invalid plans. Residual failure modes are known, not hidden.
