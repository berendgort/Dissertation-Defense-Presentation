\chapter{Distributed Agentic AI for Edge-Cloud Orchestration}
\label{ch:distributed-agentic-ai}

The preceding chapters established foundations for intelligent orchestration: Chapter~\ref{ch:adaptive-orchestration} addressed \textit{resource efficiency} through edge-deployable prediction, and Chapter~\ref{ch:attention-driven-prediction} addressed \textit{operational scalability} through cross-application generalization. Yet predictions require autonomous systems to act upon them. The complexity of \ac{6G} networks, characterized by heterogeneous resources and strict \ac{QoS} requirements, exceeds the capacity of manual management or simple rule-based systems. This chapter addresses the final challenge, \textit{validated autonomy}, by introducing \ac{AgentEdge}, a distributed agentic framework that completes the intelligent orchestration pipeline.
