## Defense Morning Plan

- **Presentation start:** 10:00
- **Arrival target:** 09:00
- **Drive from home:** about 45 minutes
- **Location:** ETSETB B3 Teleensenyament, Campus Nord UPC, Barcelona
- **Map:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/rvBNN6TQCsCRVxaF8

## Before 09:00

1. Wake up early enough to get ready without rushing.
2. Eat a simple breakfast and keep coffee / caffeine normal.
3. Do one final check before leaving:
   - laptop
   - charger
   - adapters
   - clicker
   - USB backup
   - notes sheet
   - water
   - phone
4. Leave home around **08:00**. A 45 minute drive should get you there around **08:45**, which gives a small buffer for traffic, parking, and walking to the room.
5. Drive to **Campus Nord UPC** and head to **ETSETB B3 Teleensenyament**.
6. Aim to be physically at the room by **09:00**.

## After 09:00 Until Presentation Start

1. Check in with the person who asked you to arrive early.
2. Go into the room and set up your laptop.
3. Test the slides, adapter, display connection, and clicker.
4. Keep your charger and USB backup easy to reach.
5. Put the title slide up and have the first minutes ready.
6. Go to the bathroom if needed, drink a little water, and put your phone on silent.
7. Avoid last-minute slide changes unless something is actually broken.
8. Around **09:50**, be fully ready, calm, and standing by.
9. At **10:00**, start the presentation.
